At Christmas, we experience the joy of our Lord Jesus Christ. Gift giving is a wonderful, meaningful part of that experience. Unfortunately, severe poverty deprives many children in developing countries of this joy.

This year, YOU can help provide a special blessing and Christmas joy to precious children. Mobilize your community to unite in our Catholic faith by participating in Cross Catholic Outreach’s Box of Joy ministry. Watch this video to learn more.

This fun, hands-on project empowers Catholics to share the love of Jesus as they personally shop, select and pack gifts for impoverished boys and girls.

Box of Joy week is November 3 - 11, but it’s not too early to start planning. Sign-up to become a project leader in your parish or school! Cross Catholic will send everything you need to engage your community in this work of mercy.
